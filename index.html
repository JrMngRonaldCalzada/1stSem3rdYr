About Trike Track

Trike Track is a mobile application enterprise whose goal is to offer a precise, versatile and exact fare calculator when commuting via tricycle. It follows the trend of using technological-based advancement as a tool for public commutes and turning it into a business opportunity that can benefit both the enterprise and the customer.

Mission

"The Trike Track Application offers to track commute travels via Tricycle and monitor the exact tricycle fare
to be paid, providing the Tricycle Driver and the Passenger to properly follow the Tricycle Fare Guide (as per City Ordinance No.2011-007, s. of 2011). Our team will ensure that the application will guarantee to compute the exact amount of tricycle fare to be paid."

Vision

"To make Trike Track accessible and accepted by the municipalities of Ilocos Norte."
